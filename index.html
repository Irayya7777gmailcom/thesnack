About The Snack Mill

The Snack Mill was born from a simple belief — that the best food is rooted in tradition. We craft our snacks and sweets using age-old recipes, wholesome ingredients, and a whole lot of love.

From the warm crunch of our Bhadang to the melt-in-your-mouth softness of our Besan Laddu, every product is a tribute to the rich culinary heritage of India.

Based in Bengaluru, we deliver authentic flavours across India — bringing the taste of home to your doorstep, one bite at a time.
